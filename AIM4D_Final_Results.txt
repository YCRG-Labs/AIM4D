STAGE 5: EARLY WARNING SYSTEM
======================================================================

Architecture:
  - 4 detection channels: CSD (univariate + multivariate), election
    vulnerability, democratic vulnerability, military threat
  - GDELT event z-scores (protest/conflict/repression) as coup precursors
  - V-Dem institutional erosion indicators (judicial independence)
  - Elastic net feature selection (~30/70 features retained)
  - Stacked ensemble: LogisticRegressionCV + GradientBoosting
    with cross-validated stacking weights (LR: 0.20, GB: 0.80)
  - Soft distance-weighted labels (exp decay, half-life=2yr)
  - Country-percentile features for relative risk detection
  - Tiered alerts: watch (P80), warning (P95), alert (P98)

Meta-learner innovations:
  - Multivariate CSD: dominant eigenvalue + mean cross-correlation of
    rolling factor covariance matrix (Weinans et al. 2021, Held & Kleinen 2004)
  - Kendall tau with ARMA surrogate significance (Dakos et al. 2012)
  - Soft labels give gradient information about proximity to onset
  - Country percentiles capture "this country's risk is at its historical peak"

======================================================================
KNOWN VULNERABILITIES & FRAMING
======================================================================

1. HMM BOUNDARY CASES
   Canada, Portugal, South Korea show liberal->electoral flips in 2020-2025.
   These are borderline in V-Dem's own data and reflect real measurement
   variation, not model hallucination. Asymmetric Dirichlet priors
   (diagonal=500 for liberal democracy) minimize oscillation while
   preserving genuine transitions.

2. USA 2025 CLASSIFICATION
   "The model classifies the United States as competitive authoritarian in
   2025, reflecting documented declines in V-Dem's institutional indicators.
   We note this classification is contested and falls at the boundary of the
   model's applicability to consolidated democracies undergoing rapid change."

3. CAUSAL LANGUAGE
   DO NOT USE: "causal identification"
   USE: "principled decomposition under stated assumptions"
   USE: "diffusion" and "contagion" (Gleditsch & Ward 2006 standard)
   The NSCM provides structure for decomposition, not identification.
   Without an instrument or exogenous shock to network structure, contagion
   cannot be separated from correlated shocks. Cite Manski (1993) reflection
   problem, use Forastiere et al. (2021) as aspiration, acknowledge gap.

4. V-DEM CIRCULARITY
   Three mitigations: (a) POET extracts latent factors, not raw indicators;
   (b) GDELT + World Bank features are independent of V-Dem; (c) 5-year
   temporal separation between features and outcomes. Cite V-FORE doing the
   same (Lindberg 2023). Validate against Polity V in appendix.
   "This structural feature — shared with most V-Dem-based forecasting work
   — is mitigated by latent factor extraction, multi-source features, and
   temporal separation."

5. TEMPORAL OUT-OF-SAMPLE (NOT "PROSPECTIVE")
   DO NOT USE: "prospective out-of-sample forecasting"
   USE: "temporal out-of-sample evaluation" and "expanding-window CV"
   USE: "simulated real-time evaluation" for the 2017-cutoff test
   The 2026-2031 risk ranking IS a genuine prospective forecast.
